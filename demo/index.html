Freedom.js

BRING FREEDOM to your HTML

*About*

In commemoration of July 4, 2016, Freedom.js was made to add the red, white, and blue colors of the American flag to your HTML text. You're celebrating 4th of July. Now your HTML can too!

No more boring, unpatriotic text! Freedom.js is so patriotic you can barely see it!

*How To Use*

To use Freedom.js, all you need to do is link the freedom.js and freedom.css to your HTML.
